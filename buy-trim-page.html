Sport

The A3 Saloon Sport is the standard model but it comes with a huge kit list as standard. It features 17" alloy wheels, a 5.8" screen, Audi Music Interface (iPod connections), bluetooth, DAB radio and voice control system. Inside you'll find sports seats in Rallye cloth, a 3 spoke leather sports steering wheel with multifunction controls, aluminium trim throughout and dual zone climate control. Lowered sports suspension is what this model comes with as standard, but at no cost you can opt for the more comfortable standard suspension. Also includes sat nav.
